HemeraScope

One dashboard. Complete clarity.

HemeraScope combines carbon footprint analysis, supplier ESG intelligence, and statistical uncertainty into a single, auditable deliverable.

Carbon Intelligence

Scope 1, 2 & 3 emissions calculated from your spend data

Upload your activity and spend data. Our system classifies every transaction, matches suppliers, and calculates emissions using DEFRA-verified factors. Full GHG Protocol compliance with quantified uncertainty on every number.

Data Uncertainty

Every number comes with a confidence interval

Pedigree matrix scoring across 5 dimensions. Monte Carlo uncertainty propagation. You'll know exactly how reliable each figure is — and where to invest in better data.

Supplier ESG

100+ data points. One Hemera Score per supplier

We check Companies House, Environment Agency, HSE, SBTi, CDP, and dozens more public registries. Every finding is traceable to its source. Deep analysis verifies and challenges the automated results, with benchmarking against sector peers.

Supplier Engagement

We don't just flag problems — we help fix them

Hemera works directly with your suppliers to close ESG gaps. Modern slavery statements, SBTi commitments, environmental compliance — we facilitate the conversations and track progress. Suppliers who meet our standards earn the Hemera Verified badge.

How it works

From upload to insight in days, not months. Collaboration at every step.

01

Upload your activity and spend data

A simple CSV or Excel file. We handle the complexity — every transaction is classified into GHG Protocol scopes and categories automatically.

Intelligent classification with full audit trail. Every decision is traceable.
02

We analyse your supply chain

Over 100 data points checked per supplier across government registries, certification bodies, and regulatory databases. Every finding benchmarked against sector peers.

Multi-layered intelligence with deterministic scoring and deep analysis.
03

Expert review with full transparency

Every finding is reviewed by a human analyst who can include, skip, or challenge it. We walk you through the results and explain every number.

Statistical sampling to ISO 19011 standards. Nothing goes to you unchecked.
04

You receive your HemeraScope report

Carbon footprint, supplier ESG profiles, uncertainty analysis, and recommended actions — delivered as an interactive dashboard, branded PDF, and Power BI access.

Every number traceable to its source. Every figure accompanied by quantified uncertainty.
05

We engage with your suppliers

We don't just flag problems — we contact your suppliers, facilitate conversations, and help them improve. Suppliers who meet our standards earn the Hemera Verified badge.

About Hemera Intelligence

Named after the Greek goddess of daylight, Hemera Intelligence was founded on a simple belief: supply chain sustainability should be transparent, rigorous, and accessible to all.

Because sunshine is the best disinfectant.

We combine academic rigour with cutting-edge technology to deliver supply chain intelligence that stands up to scrutiny — fast, statistically significant, and with quantified uncertainty on every number. No black-box calculations. No unexplained figures. We walk you through every result, because if you can't explain it, you can't trust it. Our approach is collaborative — we work with your suppliers, not against them — because real sustainability happens through partnership.
