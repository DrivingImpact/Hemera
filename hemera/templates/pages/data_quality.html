Data Quality Improvement

Hemera uses a six-level emission factor cascade model to select the most accurate available factor for each line item. Level 1 (supplier-specific) provides the highest precision, while Level 6 (generic fallback) carries the greatest uncertainty. Moving spend up the cascade — by obtaining supplier-specific data or switching to activity-based measurement — reduces uncertainty and improves the overall data quality grade.
