Scope Breakdown

Emissions are classified into three scopes following the GHG Protocol Corporate Standard. Scope 1 covers direct emissions from owned or controlled sources (e.g., gas boilers, company vehicles). Scope 2 covers indirect emissions from purchased electricity, heat, and steam. Scope 3 covers all other indirect emissions in the organisation's value chain, across 15 categories.
