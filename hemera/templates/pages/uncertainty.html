Uncertainty Analysis

Every emission estimate in this report carries a quantified uncertainty, calculated using the ecoinvent pedigree matrix (Ciroth et al., 2016). Each line item is scored across five data quality indicators — reliability, completeness, temporal correlation, geographical correlation, and technological correlation — each on a scale of 1 (best) to 5 (worst). These scores are combined under a lognormal distribution to produce a Geometric Standard Deviation (GSD), from which the 95% confidence interval is derived as [estimate / GSD², estimate × GSD²].
