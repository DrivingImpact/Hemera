Reduction Roadmap

The reduction roadmap presents prioritised opportunities for lowering the organisation's carbon footprint. Actions are assessed on two dimensions: estimated emission reduction potential (based on published benchmarks from the Carbon Trust, BEIS, and IEA) and implementation effort. The quadrant framework helps prioritise: Quick Wins (high impact, low effort) should be implemented immediately, while Strategic actions (high impact, high effort) require planning and investment.

Impact vs Effort

The quadrant chart plots each reduction action by its implementation effort (x-axis) against its estimated emission reduction (y-axis). Actions in the top-left quadrant are "quick wins" — high impact with low effort. Actions in the top-right are "strategic" — high impact but requiring significant investment or organisational change. Bubble size is proportional to reduction potential; colour indicates action type.
