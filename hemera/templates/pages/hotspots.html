Emission Hotspots

Hotspot analysis identifies the emission categories and spending areas that contribute most to the total footprint. This analysis guides reduction strategy by highlighting where action will have the greatest impact. Categories are coloured by scope: slate for Scope 1 (direct), teal for Scope 2 (purchased energy), and amber for Scope 3 (value chain).
