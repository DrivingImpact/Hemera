Projections are modelled in two stages: (1) data quality improvement narrows the confidence interval without necessarily changing the central estimate, and (2) emission reduction actions lower the central estimate itself. The fan chart shows both effects over a three-year horizon. The waterfall decomposes the pathway into discrete improvement steps.
